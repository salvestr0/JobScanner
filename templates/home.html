Features

Everything you need to land the job

Smart Match Scoring

Every listing is scored 0–100 against your skills, salary expectations, location, and keywords. Only relevant matches reach you.

Daily Email Digest

Your top matches delivered to your inbox each morning — clean, scannable, with one-click links to apply. No app to open.

AI Cover Notes

Gemini AI generates a tailored cover note for each top match based on your resume and the job description. One click, ready to send.

Application Tracker

Mark jobs as Applied, Interview, or Skip. Add notes, schedule interview dates, and track your whole pipeline in one place.

Analytics Dashboard

See score distributions, top hiring companies, weekly job volume trends, and your personal application funnel at a glance.

AI Scan Modes

Exploring a career switch? Switch modes — logistics, healthcare, F&B — and Gemini AI generates a fresh search profile for that field.

Resume Tailoring

Point Gemini at any job and get rewritten resume bullets and a tailored summary that emphasise exactly what that employer is looking for.

Interview Prep

AI generates role-specific technical, behavioural, and company-fit questions for any job — plus smart questions to ask the interviewer.
