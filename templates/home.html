Common questions

What job source does CareerScan use?
CareerScan uses the JSearch / OpenWeb Ninja API for job sourcing, with country filters selected inside the app. Availability can vary by country and API coverage.
How does the scoring work?
Each listing is compared against your profile — skills, salary range, preferred location, and keywords. The score (0–100) reflects how well it fits. You also see the reasons: what matched, what didn't, and whether the salary was in range.
Will it work if I'm fresh out of poly or ITE?
Yes. The scoring accounts for your education level and filters out roles that require degrees if you don't have one. You can also set keywords like "entry level", "fresh grad", or "no experience required" to boost relevant listings.
Is my data safe?
Your profile and resume are stored encrypted. We don't share or sell your data to employers or recruiters. You can delete your account and everything associated with it at any time from Settings.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your account settings with one click. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period — no early cutoff, no penalties.
