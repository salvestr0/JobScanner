FAQ

Common questions

What is MyCareersFuture?
MyCareersFuture (MCF) is Singapore's national jobs portal, run by Workforce Singapore (WSG). Employers must post their Singapore-based roles there first before listing elsewhere. All listings are verified employer postings — no ghost jobs or recruitment spam.
How does the AI match scoring work?
CareerJobScan reads each job description and compares it against your profile — skills, experience level, salary expectations, preferred locations, and keywords. Every listing gets a 0–100 score. You also see the reasons: which skills matched, what was missing, and why the salary was in or out of range.
Is my resume and profile data safe?
Yes. Your profile and resume are stored encrypted at rest. We never share or sell your data to employers, recruiters, or any third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings with one click. You keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period. No penalties, no questions asked.
